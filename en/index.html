Hey there!

My GitHub Pages are suppoed to showcase my 'weekend projects' that I've found fun and/or interesting. For me, the initial desgin of the project feels like solving puzzles and that's why I spend most of my time on this step. I think that perfected details are far less valuable than overall idea and draft. Therefore my projects are finished and functional but for sure far from impeccable.

Overall, I like to create everything myself to dig into the actual problem and tool that I am usign. Because of that I opt not to use APIs, minimize number of dependancies etc. This site for example will be pretty raw, as I've never used HTML+CSS before, not mentioning JS. Even if they are not perfect, at least I know my products inside-out. :)

Another thing, everything you see here was created by me, unless stated otherwise. That's why I'd like to acknowledge bootswatch.com/lux/ being a source of CSS that I based this site on, as well as, github.com/dwyl/ that gave me some insight into contact form creation without established server.

Okay, enough, have fun!
